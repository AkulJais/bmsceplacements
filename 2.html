Profile

Name : Dr.Akhila Mishra


Dr. Akhila Mishra was born in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh,India. Obtained her B.E in Computer Science and Engineering from Visveswaraya University in 2011. She received M.Tech in Data Science with 3rd Rank from Princeton University, USA in 2015. Her M.Tech study was supported by MHRD (Ministry of Human Resource Development) scholarship for one and half years . She completed her Ph.D program from Technical University Munich, Muenchen, Germany. Her PhD work was supported by HTRA (Half-time Research Assistantship) Scholarship for five years at Technical University Munich. In her credit, she has fifteen journal publications and fourteen international conference publications. She has presented her papers in person at Beijing and Kuala Lumpur with travel grants support from IIT Madras in Deep Learning. She received Best Data Scientist Award twice from Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) . She has been invited as a resource person for conduction of workshop on Latex and Deep Learning to faculty members of various engineering colleges. She received a grants from TEQIP-II (BMS College of Engineering) and Amoudi Scientific Research Foundation of Majmaah University, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia to conduct research in the area of Medical Thermography.

Why did you choose to study at BMSCE?
The garden and the campus life here were major selling points. The facilities in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering were also a key factor.

What are your happiest memories of your time at BMSCE?
Making new friends and beating rival colleges at Cultural fest remain fond memories! On the course, the Formula Student experience was a highlight. Overall, it was a very fulfilling time.

Can you tell us about your work placement?
For my Professional Training placement, I wanted to stick with a highly mathematical role. I worked at Mphasis and I had the chance to employ data engineering and simulation within the actual industry. I also spent several hours with my mentor during my placement. That guided my final decision about what area of engineering to enter as a career. I haven’t looked back since!
